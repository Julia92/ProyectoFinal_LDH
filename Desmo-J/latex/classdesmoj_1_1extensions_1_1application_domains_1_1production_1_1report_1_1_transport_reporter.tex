\subsection{Detailed Description}
Captures all relevant information about the {\ttfamily \doxyref{Transport\-Junction}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_transport_junction}}. That means it collects all data from the master (transporter) and the slave (goods) queue.
