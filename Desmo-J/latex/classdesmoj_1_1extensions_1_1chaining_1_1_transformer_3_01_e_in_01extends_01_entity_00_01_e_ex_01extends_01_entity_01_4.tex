\subsection{Detailed Description}
The Tranformer takes a given entitiy and transforms it into a new Type of entity. The user has to override the method \doxyref{transform\-Entity()}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1chaining_1_1_transformer_3_01_e_in_01extends_01_entity_00_01_e_ex_01extends_01_entity_01_4_a49cbe012b1faca3728d893d21ce4aa33} to define the entitiy which the transformer should emit . The Tranformer inherits from the Splitter and reduces its complexity for the new purpose. Within the Transformer executes the same behaviour like the Splitter.
