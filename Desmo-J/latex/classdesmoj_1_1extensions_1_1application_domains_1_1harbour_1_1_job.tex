\subsection{Detailed Description}
A \doxyref{Job}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_job} represents any kind of job that the {\ttfamily \doxyref{Internal\-Transporter}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_internal_transporter}} has to do in a container terminal. It has a certain origin (the place the internal transporter has to drive to to get/leave the containers(goods) and destination (the place the internal transporter has to drive to to leave/get the containers(goods). It has also a certain typ\-: import or export. \doxyref{Job}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_job} is derived from Entity.
