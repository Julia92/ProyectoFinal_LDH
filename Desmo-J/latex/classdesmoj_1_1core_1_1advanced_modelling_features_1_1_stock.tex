\subsection{Detailed Description}
\doxyref{Stock}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1advanced_modelling_features_1_1_stock} is the place where a certain kind of product can be stored by producers and retrieved by consumers. There is no difference between the units of one kind of product, the units are indistinguishable. Producers are producing products and store them using {\ttfamily \doxyref{store()}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1advanced_modelling_features_1_1_stock_a5612f98102467f9010e4c22771714c46}} in the \doxyref{Stock}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1advanced_modelling_features_1_1_stock}. Consumers make the \doxyref{Stock}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1advanced_modelling_features_1_1_stock} {\ttfamily \doxyref{retrieve()}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1advanced_modelling_features_1_1_stock_aea2b868736dea7417ae6c238416cbf60}} the products to use them up. A \doxyref{Stock}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1advanced_modelling_features_1_1_stock} is also providing a process synchronization between producers and consumers. If no or not enough units of a product are available for the consumers, they have to wait in a queue until new units are delivered by a producer. The \doxyref{Stock}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1advanced_modelling_features_1_1_stock} has a certain capacity. If the \doxyref{Stock}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1advanced_modelling_features_1_1_stock} is filled to it's capacity producers have to wait in a queue until consumers are arriving and taking units out of the \doxyref{Stock}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1advanced_modelling_features_1_1_stock}. Then the producers can fill up the freed space. This is the major difference to the \doxyref{Bin}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1advanced_modelling_features_1_1_bin}. The first sort criteria of the queues is always highest priorities first, the second queueing discipline of the underlying queues and the capacity limit of every queue can be determined by the user (default is F\-I\-F\-O and unlimited capacity). \doxyref{Stock}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1advanced_modelling_features_1_1_stock} is derived from Queue\-Based, which provides all the statistical functionality for the consumer queue. The producer queue is managed by an internal Process\-Queue.

Activates the first process waiting in the consumer queue.
