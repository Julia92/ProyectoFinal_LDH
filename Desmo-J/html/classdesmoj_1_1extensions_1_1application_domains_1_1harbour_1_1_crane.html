Detailed Description

A Crane represents any kind of cranes (containerbridges, gantry crane) which loads/unloads containers(goods) onto/from an external/internal transporter or into/from yard in a container terminal. Crane is derived from SimProcess. Its lifeCycle() must be implemented to spezify the behavior of the Crane.
