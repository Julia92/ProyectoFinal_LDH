\subsection{Detailed Description}
A slightly modified version of Rotation\-Interpolator. It doesn't need much rotational data at the initiation. The alpha, rotation axis and the angles just need to be specified at the start. If the rotation is finished, the calculation will be stopped and the alpha removed.
