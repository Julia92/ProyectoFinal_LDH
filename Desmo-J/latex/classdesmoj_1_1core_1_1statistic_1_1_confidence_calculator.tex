\subsection{Detailed Description}
The {\ttfamily \doxyref{Confidence\-Calculator}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1statistic_1_1_confidence_calculator}} class has the same functionality as the {\ttfamily \doxyref{Tally}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1statistic_1_1_tally}} class. Additionally, it calculates a confidence interval for the mean of the samples passed to the {\ttfamily \doxyref{Confidence\-Calculator}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1statistic_1_1_confidence_calculator}}. Note that the confidence interval computation assumes that the samples are (approximately) identically and independently distributed. Observe that the confidence interval estimations of this class are meaningless should this assumption not hold!
