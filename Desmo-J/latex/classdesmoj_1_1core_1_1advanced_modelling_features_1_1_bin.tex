\subsection{Detailed Description}
\doxyref{Bin}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1advanced_modelling_features_1_1_bin} is the place where producers can store their products for consumers to come and use them up. \doxyref{Bin}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1advanced_modelling_features_1_1_bin} is used to implement process synchronization between producers and consumers. Producers are producing products and store them using {\ttfamily \doxyref{store()}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1advanced_modelling_features_1_1_bin_a34bc2376bb727011ec4b10bec4c9f347}} in the bin. Consumers make the \doxyref{Bin}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1advanced_modelling_features_1_1_bin} {\ttfamily deliver()} the products to use them up. If no or not enough units of a product are available for the consumers, they have to wait in a queue until new units are delivered by a producer. The first sort criteria of the queue is always highest priorities first, the second queueing discipline of the underlying queue and the capacity limit can be determined by the user (default is F\-I\-F\-O and unlimited capacity). \doxyref{Bin}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1advanced_modelling_features_1_1_bin} is derived from Queue\-Based, which provides all the statistical functionality for a queue.
