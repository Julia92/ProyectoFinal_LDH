\subsection{Detailed Description}
\doxyref{Berth}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_berth} is the place where ships are berthing until they will be unloaded and/or loaded. {\ttfamily \doxyref{Ship}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_ship}} can come and {\ttfamily \doxyref{take()}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_berth_a01e699c6aa783df670583051be14c1d0}} the needed length for his berthing. Each ship has to release the same length of the berth it once has acquired by calling the {\ttfamily \doxyref{release()}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_berth_a4d9f4be7d046d4c7d3fc57c6fb4e0e57}} method of the \doxyref{Berth}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_berth}. \doxyref{Berth}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_berth} is used to implement process synchronization between ships, which are using berth. The berth has a limited ccertain length. A ship can take the needed for him length to berth. After his unloading and/or loading the ship must release the taken length to make her available to other ships. If a ship can not get the needed length (enough free place) for his berthing, it has to wait in a queue until enough length ( free palce) are released by other ships. A ship can release its berthing length anytime. After the berht has {\ttfamily \doxyref{release()}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_berth_a4d9f4be7d046d4c7d3fc57c6fb4e0e57}} the used length the waiting queue is checked for ships waiting for her. The first sort criteria of the queue is always highest priorities first, the second queueing discipline of the underlying queue and the capacity limit can be determined by the user (default is F\-I\-F\-O and unlimited capacity).
