\subsection{Detailed Description}
A \doxyref{C\-\_\-\-Control}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_c___control} represents the control component for the cranes. The cranes and the transporters (external/internal) can activate (interrupt) the crane control to begin with their serving. Its {\ttfamily \doxyref{life\-Cycle()}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_c___control_a54c1fbb7a4cffd9d4769644177262cad}} is already implemented and spezifies the behavior of the crane control.
