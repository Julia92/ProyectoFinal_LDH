\subsection{Detailed Description}
\doxyref{Unloading}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_unloading} is the object representing the process cooperation between a {\ttfamily \doxyref{Internal\-Transporter}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_internal_transporter}} or {\ttfamily \doxyref{Crane}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_crane}} process and the \doxyref{Truck}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_truck} or an Internal Transporter he is unloading. It is intended that this \doxyref{Unloading}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_unloading} is used with the {\ttfamily Wait\-Queue} construct, where a {\ttfamily Inernal\-Transporter} or {\ttfamily \doxyref{Crane}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_crane}}$>$( as a master process) is waiting for a truck or an internal transporter to unload it. During the \doxyref{Unloading}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_unloading} the master is active and the slaves is passive. The unloading carried out together is described in the method {\ttfamily cooperation}, which can be overwritten by the user to build more complex models. Until now this method only models the time it takes to unload the truck/ an internal transporter.

This method describes the unloading process carried out by the master process (some kind of {\ttfamily \doxyref{Internal\-Transporter}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_internal_transporter}} or {\ttfamily \doxyref{Crane}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_crane}}). In this simple case only the time it takes to unload the containers (goods) is taken into consideration. If the user building the model has to implement a more complex behavior, he has to overwrite this method in a subclass. The time it takes to unload the containers (goods) is obtained from the method {\ttfamily \doxyref{get\-Unload\-Time\-Sample()}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_unloading_a148be8b3d4901842da1c836105164fb1}}.
