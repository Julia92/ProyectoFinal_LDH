\subsection{Detailed Description}
\doxyref{Entrepot}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_entrepot} is some kind of storage where products (in the form of {\ttfamily Sim\-Process} es) can be stored. They will be stored in a kind of F\-I\-F\-O order, that means the products arriving first at the \doxyref{Entrepot}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_entrepot} will be the first ones to be removed. One can remove products from the \doxyref{Entrepot}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_entrepot} in order to sell (or dispatch) them or to process them in any other way. Note\-: while products can enter the \doxyref{Entrepot}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_entrepot} themselves, another process ( {\ttfamily Sim\-Process}) is needed to remove the products from the \doxyref{Entrepot}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_entrepot}. Because the product processes are passivated while they are stored in the Entepot! The \doxyref{Entrepot}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_entrepot} has no capacity limit, so it can store (almost) an endless number of products. The maximum number of available products in the \doxyref{Entrepot}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_entrepot} will be shown in the report and can be used to judge what a reasonable capacity limit for the \doxyref{Entrepot}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_entrepot} in the real world might be. In contrast the queue for the waiting customers can have a certain limit. If the customers' queue capacity is reached any further customer will be rejected (refused to service). The default number of products an \doxyref{Entrepot}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_entrepot} starts with is zero. It will be filled during simulation by some manufacturing process (or the product process itself) using the {\ttfamily \doxyref{store\-Product()}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_entrepot_ac26a75bf8aec42a4914b137602ce26ea}} method. Products can be retrieved (removed) from the \doxyref{Entrepot}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_entrepot} using one of the {\ttfamily \doxyref{remove\-Product()}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_entrepot_a704154f6902edf37de067caddd5e858c}} methods. If no or not enough products are available the costumers have to wait in a queue until new products are stored in the \doxyref{Entrepot}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_entrepot}. The first sort criteria of the customer queue is always highest priorities first. The second queueing discipline and the capacity limit of the customer queue can be determined by the user (default is F\-I\-F\-O and unlimited capacity). As long as the products (Sim\-Processes) are stored in the \doxyref{Entrepot}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_entrepot} they are passivated and blocked. \doxyref{Entrepot}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_entrepot} is derived from Queue\-Based, which provides all the statistical functionality for the customer queue.
