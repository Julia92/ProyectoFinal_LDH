\subsection{Detailed Description}
The \doxyref{Gates}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_gates} is a place on a container terminal where the {\ttfamily \doxyref{Truck}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_truck}} s enter and leave the terminal. It has a ceratin number of the entrance and exit lanes. Both must be not negative. The lanes are implemented as {\ttfamily Res} with F\-I\-F\-O-\/strategy and unlimited capacity. The \doxyref{Gates}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_gates} is derived from Model\-Component.
