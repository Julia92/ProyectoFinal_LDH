\subsection{Detailed Description}
\doxyref{Transportation}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_transportation} is the object representing the process cooperation between a {\ttfamily \doxyref{Transporter}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_transporter}} process and the goods (products represented by Sim-\/processes) he is transporting. It is intended that that this \doxyref{Transportation}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_transportation} is used with the {\ttfamily \doxyref{Transport\-Junction}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_transport_junction}} construct, where a {\ttfamily \doxyref{Transporter}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_transporter}}( as a master process) is waiting for goods (slave processes) to carry them around in the manufacturing system. During the \doxyref{Transportation}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_transportation} the master is active and the slaves are passive. The transportation carried out together is described in the method {\ttfamily cooperation}, which can be overwritten by the user to build more complex models. Until now this method only models the time it takes to transport the goods. Note\-: when using the {\ttfamily \doxyref{Transport\-Junction}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_transport_junction}} construct the master (\doxyref{Transporter}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_transporter}) will be activated after the transportation is done and the slaves will be activated after the master (if they have not been activated during the transportation already).
