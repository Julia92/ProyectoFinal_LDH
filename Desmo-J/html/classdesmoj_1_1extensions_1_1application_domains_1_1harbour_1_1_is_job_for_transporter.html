Detailed Description

This condition should check if the job for the internal transporter (VC) has as the origin or destination the Crane which that internal transporter (VC) is assigned to. The condition is used by the FixedVCtoCBAssignment_Strategy to check if there'is a job for a VC that is assigned a certain Crane to.
