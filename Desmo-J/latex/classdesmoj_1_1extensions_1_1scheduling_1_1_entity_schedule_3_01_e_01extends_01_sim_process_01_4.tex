\subsection{Detailed Description}
Entity\-Schedule is a list of \doxyref{Entity\-Schedule\-Entry}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1scheduling_1_1_entity_schedule_entry}'s. A \doxyref{Entity\-Schedule\-Entry}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1scheduling_1_1_entity_schedule_entry} is a time region (e.\-g. every workingday from 8\-:00 until 16\-:00) All \doxyref{Entity\-Schedule\-Entry}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1scheduling_1_1_entity_schedule_entry}'s have the same timezone, stored in Entity\-Schedule.

Entity\-Schedule is a Sim\-Process. With set\-Process is a process specified. The lifecycle of Entity\-Schedule will activate the with set\-Process specified process at begin and end of each schedule entry.
