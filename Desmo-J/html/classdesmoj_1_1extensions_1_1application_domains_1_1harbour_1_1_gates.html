Detailed Description

The Gates is a place on a container terminal where the Truck s enter and leave the terminal. It has a ceratin number of the entrance and exit lanes. Both must be not negative. The lanes are implemented as Res with FIFO-strategy and unlimited capacity. The Gates is derived from ModelComponent.
