\subsection{Detailed Description}
The basic interface of the \doxyref{Spatial\-Object}{p.}{interfacedesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1space3_d_1_1_spatial_object}. It contains simple setting and getting methods of coordinates for every visible object in the simulation.  Every \doxyref{Spatial\-Object}{p.}{interfacedesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1space3_d_1_1_spatial_object} may contain several or none entry or exit point. They define the specific coordinates where an \doxyref{Movable\-Spatial\-Object}{p.}{interfacedesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1space3_d_1_1_movable_spatial_object} move to or get out, if they navigate into or out of this \doxyref{Spatial\-Object}{p.}{interfacedesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1space3_d_1_1_spatial_object}.
