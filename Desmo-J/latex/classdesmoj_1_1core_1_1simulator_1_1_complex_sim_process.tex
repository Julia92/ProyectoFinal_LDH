\subsection{Detailed Description}
A {\ttfamily \doxyref{Complex\-Sim\-Process}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1simulator_1_1_complex_sim_process}} is a {\ttfamily \doxyref{Sim\-Process}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1simulator_1_1_sim_process}} which serves as a container for other {\ttfamily \doxyref{Sim\-Process}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1simulator_1_1_sim_process}} es. A {\ttfamily \doxyref{Complex\-Sim\-Process}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1simulator_1_1_complex_sim_process}} has its own lifecycle. As long as this {\ttfamily \doxyref{Complex\-Sim\-Process}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1simulator_1_1_complex_sim_process}} is active all its contained Simprocesses are passive. That means they are blocked and can not proceed in their life\-Cycles.

This will be done for all Complex\-Sim\-Porcesses recursively, until all simple Sim\-Processes are removed. The Sim\-Processes being removed from the \doxyref{Complex\-Sim\-Process}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1simulator_1_1_complex_sim_process} will be activated after the current \doxyref{Sim\-Process}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1simulator_1_1_sim_process} so they can follow their own life\-Cycle again. Of course, this does only make sense for Sim\-Processes which are not terminated already.
