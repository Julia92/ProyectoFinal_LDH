\subsection{Detailed Description}
A message which a process can send to a \doxyref{Message\-Crossbar}{p.}{} or a \doxyref{Message\-Channel}{p.}{} to activate processes waiting on the them. The \doxyref{Crossbar\-Message}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1crossbar_1_1_crossbar_message} contains the \doxyref{Time\-Instant}{p.}{} on which it was sent and its sender (the process that send the message to the crossbar).\par
 \par
 Subclasses of the \doxyref{Crossbar\-Message}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1crossbar_1_1_crossbar_message} can be used to transport information between senders and receivers of messages.
