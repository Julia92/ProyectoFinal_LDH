\subsection{Detailed Description}
Class to plot Desmo\-J histogram, histogram\-Accumulater and time-\/series data in j\-Free\-Chart \doxyref{Plotter}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1grafic_1_1util_1_1_plotter}. When in Desmo\-J dataset get\-Show\-Time\-Spans\-In\-Report() is set, the data values are interpreted as a timespan in a appropriate time unit. The Desmo\-J datasets are converted in j\-Free\-Chart Format. Onscreen and offscreen plots are supported.

See also \doxyref{Paint\-Panel}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1grafic_1_1util_1_1_paint_panel} and \doxyref{Time\-Series\-Data\-Set\-Adapter}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1grafic_1_1util_1_1_time_series_data_set_adapter}
