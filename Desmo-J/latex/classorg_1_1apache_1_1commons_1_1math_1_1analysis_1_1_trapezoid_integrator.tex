\subsection{Detailed Description}
Implements the {\tt Trapezoidal Rule} for integration of real univariate functions. For reference, see {\bfseries Introduction to Numerical Analysis}, I\-S\-B\-N 038795452\-X, chapter 3. 

The function should be integrable.
