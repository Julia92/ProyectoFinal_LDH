\subsection{Detailed Description}
A \doxyref{Machine\-Process}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_machine_process} represents any kind of machine which is running at some {\ttfamily \doxyref{Work\-Station}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_work_station}} and processing parts (products or goods) there. \doxyref{Machine\-Process}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_machine_process} is derived from Sim\-Process. Its {\ttfamily \doxyref{life\-Cycle()}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_machine_process_a5cd68ddb2cb8191918c4284c343e2e15}} must be implemented by the user in order to specify the behavior of the \doxyref{Machine\-Process}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_machine_process}.
