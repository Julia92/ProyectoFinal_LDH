\subsection{Detailed Description}
A \doxyref{Transporter}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_transporter} represents any kind of vehicle or conveyor belt which carries goods (products) around in a manufacturing system. It has a certain capacity (maximum number of goods which can be carried around at once) and a minimum load (minimum number of goods which will be carried). \doxyref{Transporter}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_transporter} is derived from Sim\-Process. Its {\ttfamily \doxyref{life\-Cycle()}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_transporter_a28be8efaf796c5643c95edc7b6db744b}} must be implemented by the user in order to specify the behavior of the \doxyref{Transporter}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_transporter}.
