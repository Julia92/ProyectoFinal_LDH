\subsection{Detailed Description}
\doxyref{Processing}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_processing} is the object representing the processing process taking place at a {\ttfamily \doxyref{Work\-Station}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_work_station}} where a {\ttfamily \doxyref{Worker}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_worker}} process or a {\ttfamily \doxyref{Machine\-Process}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_machine_process}} are processing some parts (products) to manufacture some new parts (or products). The kind and the number of parts being processed are determined by a {\ttfamily \doxyref{Parts\-List}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_parts_list}}. The parts (or products) are represented by {\ttfamily Sim\-Process} es. The {\ttfamily \doxyref{Worker}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_worker}} and the {\ttfamily \doxyref{Machine\-Process}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_machine_process}} are also some kind of {\ttfamily Sim\-Process} es. But one {\ttfamily \doxyref{Worker}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_worker}} or {\ttfamily \doxyref{Machine\-Process}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_machine_process}} is the master process and the other processes are all slave processes. It is intended that that this \doxyref{Processing}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_processing} is used with the {\ttfamily \doxyref{Work\-Station}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_work_station}} construct, where a master process ( {\ttfamily \doxyref{Worker}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_worker}} or {\ttfamily \doxyref{Machine\-Process}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_machine_process}}) is waiting for parts or products (slave processes) to process them. During the \doxyref{Processing}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_processing} the master process is active and the slave processes are passive. The processing taking place at the {\ttfamily \doxyref{Work\-Station}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_work_station}} is described in the method {\ttfamily cooperation}, which should be overwritten by the user to model the manufacturing process taking place at the {\ttfamily \doxyref{Work\-Station}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_work_station}}. Until now this method only models the time it takes to process the parts in some way. Note\-: when using the {\ttfamily \doxyref{Work\-Station}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_work_station}} construct the master (\doxyref{Worker}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_worker} or \doxyref{Machine\-Process}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_machine_process}) will be activated after the processing is done and the slave processes will be activated after the master (if they have not been activated during the processing process already).
