\subsection{Detailed Description}
Resources are objects needed by Sim\-Processes to perform certain tasks. Every \doxyref{Resource}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1simulator_1_1_resource} has its unique I\-D number, so it can be identified. Each \doxyref{Resource}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1simulator_1_1_resource} belongs to a certain resource category (resource pool) and has the status ready (can be used) or {\ttfamily out\-Of\-Order} if it is broken down.
