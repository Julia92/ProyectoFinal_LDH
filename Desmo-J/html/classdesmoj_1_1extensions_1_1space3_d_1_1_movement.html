Detailed Description

The Movement class stores the track, the duration informations of each phase of a movement and its associated speed. A movement will be divided into 3 phases, the acceleration phase, the maximum speed phase and the deceleration phase. It's allowed to set the initial speed greater than the maximum speed. Then the acceleration phase will be used to reduce the speed to the maximum speed.
