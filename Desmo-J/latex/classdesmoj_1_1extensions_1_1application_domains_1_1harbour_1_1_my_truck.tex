\subsection{Detailed Description}
This condition should check if the number of lane of the truck where he waits for an internal transporter at the {\ttfamily \doxyref{Holding\-Area}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_holding_area}} is equal the number of the lane of this condition. The condition is used by an internal transporter to find the needed truck that it has to unload/load.
