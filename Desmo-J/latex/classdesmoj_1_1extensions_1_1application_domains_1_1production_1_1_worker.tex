\subsection{Detailed Description}
A \doxyref{Worker}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_worker} represents any kind of human worker who is working at some {\ttfamily \doxyref{Work\-Station}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_work_station}} and processing parts (products or goods) there. \doxyref{Worker}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_worker} is derived from Sim\-Process. Its {\ttfamily \doxyref{life\-Cycle()}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_worker_aa41851ecb521d763f60dcd8ffb61d80b}} must be implemented by the user in order to specify the behavior of the \doxyref{Worker}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_worker}.
