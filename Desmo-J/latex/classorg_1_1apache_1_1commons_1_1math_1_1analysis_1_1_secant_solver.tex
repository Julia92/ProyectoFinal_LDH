\subsection{Detailed Description}
Implements a modified version of the {\tt secant method} for approximating a zero of a real univariate function. 

The algorithm is modified to maintain bracketing of a root by successive approximations. Because of forced bracketing, convergence may be slower than the unrestricted secant algorithm. However, this implementation should in general outperform the {\tt regula falsi method.}

The function is assumed to be continuous but not necessarily smooth.
