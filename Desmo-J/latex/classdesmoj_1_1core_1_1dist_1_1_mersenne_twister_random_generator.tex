\subsection{Detailed Description}
Mersenne twister uniform pseudo random generator configured such that a stream of [0,1] double values is produced. Implements the {\ttfamily desmoj.\-dist.\-Uniform\-Random\-Generator} interface. The algorithm for pseudo random number generation is based on the Mersenne twister variant M\-T19937 and has a period of 2$^{\mbox{19937}}$  -\/ 1; see {\tt the Mersenne twister wikipedia entry} for details.
