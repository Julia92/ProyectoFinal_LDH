\subsection{Detailed Description}
A \doxyref{T\-\_\-\-Control}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_t___control} represents the control component for the transporters. The transporters get the {\ttfamily Jobs} from this control that assignes the job to them by using a certain {\ttfamily \doxyref{Transport\-Strategy}{p.}{interfacedesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_transport_strategy}}. Its {\ttfamily \doxyref{life\-Cycle()}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_t___control_ac09656e2e4e7e0ce409df7a49ca9a234}} is already implemented and spezifies the behavior of the transport control.
