\subsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a message channel which resides on a \doxyref{Message\-Crossbar}{p.}{}. Message\-Channels can only be used in conjunction with a message crossbar.\par
 \par
 On the one hand Message\-Channels allow for processes to wait for \doxyref{Crossbar\-Message}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1crossbar_1_1_crossbar_message}s on them (passivating the process) and on the other hand they allow for processes to send Crossbar\-Messages to them. If a \doxyref{Crossbar\-Message}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1crossbar_1_1_crossbar_message} is received this leads to the immediate activation of all process waiting on the channel.
