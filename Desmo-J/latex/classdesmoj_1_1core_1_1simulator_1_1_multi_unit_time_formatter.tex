\subsection{Detailed Description}
Use this class to format \doxyref{Time\-Span}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1simulator_1_1_time_span} and \doxyref{Time\-Instant}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1simulator_1_1_time_instant} objects like in the following examples\-: 59 Minutes, 28 Seconds, and 548 Milliseconds could be formatted like O\-:59\-:28\-:548 or 59\-:28 or 59\-:28\-:548\-:000\-:000 or 0.\-59.\-28.
