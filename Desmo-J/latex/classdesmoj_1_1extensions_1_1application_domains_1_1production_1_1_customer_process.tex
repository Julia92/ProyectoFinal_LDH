\subsection{Detailed Description}
\doxyref{Customer\-Process}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_customer_process} is a kind of process representing a customer. He is fetching a certain number of products from an {\ttfamily \doxyref{Entrepot}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_entrepot}} to use them up. He is not doing anything special with them and so the products will be collected by the garbage collector. To change this behavior overwrite the method {\ttfamily \doxyref{life\-Cycle()}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_customer_process_ad56eda35ad6e3039d4f9f105b44b42c9}} in a subclass. The \doxyref{Customer\-Process}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_customer_process} can be viewed as the border of the simulated system, where the products disappear. It is intended to be used in conjunction with the {\ttfamily \doxyref{Demand\-Process}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_demand_process}}. \doxyref{Customer\-Process}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_customer_process} has two main parameters to be specified (see the Constructor)\-: The {\ttfamily \doxyref{Entrepot}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_entrepot}} where the products are to be fetched from and the number of demanded products.

The \doxyref{Customer\-Process}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_customer_process} is fetching the given number of products from the given {\ttfamily \doxyref{Entrepot}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_entrepot}}. As there are not enough products available at the moment this \doxyref{Customer\-Process}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_customer_process} has to wait in a queue in the {\ttfamily \doxyref{Entrepot}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_entrepot}}. The obtained products are consumed (used up), that means all references to them are released and the product Sim-\/process is activated (as the process is not terminated yet), to end its life\-Cycle and terminate by itself. Then the garbage collector can delete it. Therefore the {\ttfamily Complex\-Sim\-Process} es are disassembled automatically.
