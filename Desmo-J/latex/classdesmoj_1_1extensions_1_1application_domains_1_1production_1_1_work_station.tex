\subsection{Detailed Description}
A \doxyref{Work\-Station}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_work_station} is the place, where products (parts) are processed by a machine or parts are assembled by a worker or machine. The list of parts needed for this process are provided by means of a {\ttfamily \doxyref{Parts\-List}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_parts_list}}.

Constructor for a \doxyref{Work\-Station}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_work_station}. Actually there are two waiting-\/queues constructed, one internal {\ttfamily Queue\-List} for the masters (like {\ttfamily \doxyref{Worker}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_worker}} s or {\ttfamily \doxyref{Machine\-Process}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_machine_process}} es) and one separate {\ttfamily Process\-Queue} for the slave processes (the parts or goods or products). The queueing discipline and the capacity limit of the underlying queues can be chosen. Highest priority are always first in the queues. All the slave wait queues will have the same queueing discipline and capacity limit as provided in the parameters {\ttfamily s\-Sort\-Order} and {\ttfamily s\-Q\-Capacity}. To choose individual settings for each slave wait queue use the method {\ttfamily \doxyref{set\-S\-Queue\-Capacity()}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_work_station_adb65c80f587dbd1e792c8eaf63a67c8e}} to change its capacity and/or the method {\ttfamily \doxyref{set\-S\-Queue\-Strategy()}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_work_station_a66432897a028d39429d41e03b51b1863}} to change its sort order.

This method is called from a sim-\/process (part or product) which wants to be processed at this \doxyref{Work\-Station}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1production_1_1_work_station} as a slave. If no suitable master process and enough other slave processes are available at the moment, this slave process will be stored in its slave queue, until a suitable master and enough other parts (or products) are available to start the manufacturing (processing) process. If the capacity limit of tis slave queue is reached, the process will not be enqueued and {\ttfamily false} returned. During the cooperation the master process is the only active one. The slave processes are passive and will be reactivated after the cooperation is done.
