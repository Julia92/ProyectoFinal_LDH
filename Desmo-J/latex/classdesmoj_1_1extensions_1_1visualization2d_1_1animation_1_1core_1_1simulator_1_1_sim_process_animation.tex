\subsection{Detailed Description}
Animation of a Sim\-Process. It extends from Sim\-Process. A Sim\-Process is animated by an entity. Free and static entities are supported. Free Entities can be included in Containers, as Queues, Processes, ect. and have no fixed location. Static Entities have a fixed location and can not be included in Containers, as Queues, Processes, ect.
