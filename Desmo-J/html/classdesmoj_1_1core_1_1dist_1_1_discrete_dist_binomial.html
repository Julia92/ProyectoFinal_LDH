Detailed Description

Distribution returning binomial distributed long values. The binomial distribution describes the probability of having a certain amount of successes in a series of indepedent Bernoulli experiments, all having the same success probability.

Creates a stream of pseudo random numbers following a binomial distribution. The specific parameters p (probability) and n (amount) have to be given here at creation time.
