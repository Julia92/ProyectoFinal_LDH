\subsection{Detailed Description}
A \doxyref{Crane}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_crane} represents any kind of cranes (containerbridges, gantry crane) which loads/unloads containers(goods) onto/from an external/internal transporter or into/from yard in a container terminal. \doxyref{Crane}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_crane} is derived from Sim\-Process. Its {\ttfamily \doxyref{life\-Cycle()}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1simulator_1_1_sim_process_a38bb9fa8a520a8bd0bd26e74737fb3a3}} must be implemented to spezify the behavior of the \doxyref{Crane}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_crane}.
