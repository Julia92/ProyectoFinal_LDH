\subsection{Detailed Description}
This condition should check if the job for the internal transporter (V\-C) has as the origin or destination the \doxyref{Crane}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_crane} which that internal transporter (V\-C) is assigned to. The condition is used by the {\ttfamily \doxyref{Fixed\-V\-Cto\-C\-B\-Assignment\-\_\-\-Strategy}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_fixed_v_cto_c_b_assignment___strategy}} to check if there'is a job for a V\-C that is assigned a certain \doxyref{Crane}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_crane} to.
