\subsection{Detailed Description}
An entity is a basic-\/object of animation. Every entity has an entity-\/type. Basic-\/properties of an entity e.\-g. possible attributes or states are definied in entity-\/type. The history of all values are stored also. All grafical properies are stored in Entity\-Grafic
