\subsection{Detailed Description}
The {\ttfamily \doxyref{Tally}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1core_1_1statistic_1_1_tally}} class is providing a statistic analysis about one value. The mean value and the standard deviation is calculated on basis of the total number of observations. \par
