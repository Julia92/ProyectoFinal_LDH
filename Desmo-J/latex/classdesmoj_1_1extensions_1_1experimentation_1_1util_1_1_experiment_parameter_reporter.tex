\subsection{Detailed Description}
A reporter for the model and experiment parameters of a certain experiment run represented by an experiment runner. Returned by experiment\-Runner.\-get\-Reporter(). The group\-I\-D of experiment reporters is 10 which makes the appear in the report as one of the last elements.
