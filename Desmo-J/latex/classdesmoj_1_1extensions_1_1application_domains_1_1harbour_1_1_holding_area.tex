\subsection{Detailed Description}
The \doxyref{Holding\-Area}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_holding_area} is the place where {\ttfamily \doxyref{Internal\-Transporter}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_internal_transporter}}s serve (load/unload) {\ttfamily \doxyref{Truck}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_truck}} s at a container terminal. So what happens at the \doxyref{Holding\-Area}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_holding_area} is some kind of Rendezvous synchronisation, where {\ttfamily \doxyref{Internal\-Transporter}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_internal_transporter}} s meet the {\ttfamily \doxyref{Truck}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_truck}}s to load/unload them. There is one wait queue for the masters ({\ttfamily \doxyref{Internal\-Transporter}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_internal_transporter}}s) and one queue for the slaves ( {\ttfamily \doxyref{Truck}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_truck}} s), where they have to wait for each other to cooperate. The {\ttfamily \doxyref{Internal\-Transporter}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_internal_transporter}} s are the masters which perform the loading/unloading operation. The corporate loading/unloading operation is described in the method {\ttfamily cooperation} in a subclass of {\ttfamily \doxyref{Loading}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_loading}} or {\ttfamily \doxyref{Unloading}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_unloading}}. The {\ttfamily \doxyref{Truck}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_truck}} s loaded/unloaded as slaves keep still during the loading/unloading operation and will be reactivated thereafter. The first sort criteria for the queues is always highest priorities first, the second queueing discipline of the underlying queues and the capacity limit can be determined by the user (default is F\-I\-F\-O and unlimited capacity). \doxyref{Holding\-Area}{p.}{classdesmoj_1_1extensions_1_1application_domains_1_1harbour_1_1_holding_area} is derived from {\ttfamily Wait\-Queue} which in turn is derived from {\ttfamily Queue\-Based}, which provides all the statistical functionality for the queues.
