\subsection{Detailed Description}
Implements the {\tt Romberg Algorithm} for integration of real univariate functions. For reference, see {\bfseries Introduction to Numerical Analysis}, I\-S\-B\-N 038795452\-X, chapter 3. 

Romberg integration employs k successvie refinements of the trapezoid rule to remove error terms less than order O(N$^\wedge$(-\/2k)). Simpson's rule is a special case of k = 2.
